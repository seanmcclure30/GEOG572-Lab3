Baroque Style Web Map

Made by Sean McClure

The goal of my submission for the Lab 3 assignment was to compose a web map in the style of 17th century Baroque paintings using Mapbox Studio. I took inspiration from the Flemish artist Clara Peeters who primarily created beautiful still life oil paintings depicting dining tables with different assortments of decadent meals common for aristocratic living of the time period. Clara Peeters' works were notable for her mastery of the Baroque style and for being one of the earliest artists to include hidden self-portraits usually reflecting in the brass or glass subjects in her paintings. Her use of rich dark tones accented by sharp light colors is an aesthetic I aimed to replicate in this web map. I hope you will enjoy exploring the included map.
